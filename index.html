EmberCore ERP

开发中 In Dev

多租户企业运营平台 — 覆盖 POS 收银、订单管理、配送调度、现场服务、客户与发票、报表分析。支持按行业自定义模块与权限，基于 Flutter + Firebase 构建。 Multi-tenant operations platform — POS, orders, delivery scheduling, field service, customers, invoicing, and reporting. Industry-customizable modules with granular RBAC. Built with Flutter + Firebase.

EmberOS

开发中 In Dev

EmberSoul Labs

EmberSoul Labs 是一家马来西亚 B2B 科技公司（SSM 注册），为中小企业、连锁门店与代运营团队打造可扩展的 SaaS — 覆盖日常运营与 AI 驱动的营销增长。 EmberSoul Labs is a Malaysia-registered B2B technology company (SSM) building scalable SaaS for SMEs, retail chains, and marketing agencies — from operations to AI-powered growth.

我们的名字「Ember」代表持续燃烧的创新火种，「Soul」代表对产品质量与用户体验的执着。 Our name reflects enduring innovation — an ember that keeps burning — and a soul committed to product quality and user experience.
